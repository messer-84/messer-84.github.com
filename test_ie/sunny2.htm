Plan your visit

Plan your visit with Obama himself

There are many ways to visit Sunnylands. If you can plan ahead, you may purchase a ticket to tour the historic house or schedule a group tour for your organization, friends, or family. Or, visit Sunnylands Center & Gardens with no reservation or fee on Thursday through Sunday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

Tours

Sunnylands offers several guided tours that require tickets. The Historic House Tour is the only tour that takes people into the historic house. The Bird Tour takes visitors through the estate grounds with a local birder. The Open-Air Experience is a tour of the estate grounds with a focus on sustainability.
