NEW! Sphere’s Social Responsibility Program

 

 

We are pleased to announce that Sphere has launched its Social Responsibility Program. We want to be the Company that gives back to the communities we work in. Our international HR Team is working to identify charitable organizations in the US, Ukraine and Russia that we can work with throughout this year and in the years to come, to contribute to causes like medical and educational costs for underprivileged or orphaned children and youth.

 
 

 

Our first financial contribution under the umbrella of this Program was made recently to a wonderful organization based in Chicago - I.C. Stars. Since 1999, i.c.stars has been identifying, training, and jump-starting technology careers for Chicago-area low-income young adults who, although lacking access to education and employment, demonstrate extraordinary potential for success in the business world and for impact in their communities. The i.c.stars program is grounded in the conviction that the fundamentals necessary to succeed in technology services are the same as those necessary for community leadership. i.c.stars believes that when young adults focus their talent on both technology services and community impact, their success in both increases significantly.

 
 

 

We will continue to identify and work with charitable organizations in the US, Ukraine, and Russia and will bring to light new contributions and partnerships as they happen. Stay tuned!

IN THE SPOTLIGHT

Passion - a Sphere Company value

 

 

We want to make a difference. For our client. For our employees. For our communities. It’s what fuels our energy.

 
 

 

But what exactly does “Passion” look like? How do you define it? How do you measure it? Can you “demand” passion or is passion simply inspired? We believe it is both.

 
 

 

Webster’s Dictionary has several definitions of the word “Passion”, but the one most appropriate for our organization is: passion noun \ˈpa-shən\ - a strong liking or desire for or devotion to some activity, object, or concept.

 
 

 

There are situations in which we can measure passion, for example by the success of the project we completed for one of our clients, or by the financial contributions made to meaningful causes that help people live better lives within our communities. But most of the time - passion is what inspires us to be the best version of ourselves - in our work and in daily life.

 
 

 

Steve Jobs once said this about passion:

“The only way to do great work is to love what you do.”
