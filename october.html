SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT NEWS

 

 

We continue to innovate and improve our internal tools and automations, and while our @hiringbot is waiting for the latest version of https://betterinterviews.com/ to be rolled out, we are moving forward.

 
 

 

This month we are working to introduce our new slackbot for @smm. It will help us grow our presence on Social Media and share ideas, best practices, news, links and other information with ease.

 
 

 

How it works:
The @smm bot creates a draft post in https://amplifr.com/ when the approver adds a +1 reaction to the message in Slack. That draft is then ready to be scheduled and posted across our social networks simultaneously.

 
 

 

While Development for this project is nearing completion, we are always interested in your thoughts and improvement ideas.
Development by @vmarkevich,
Product owners: @ashemerey, @vmarkevich
Source code: https://github.com/SphereSoftware/smm
