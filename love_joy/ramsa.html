LOVEJOY WHARF

Welcome to Lovejoy Wharf

Inspired by the city's tradition of red-brick with stacked bay windows, Lovejoy Wharf was designed by Robert A.M. Stern to pay homage to Boston's historic architecture and waterfront heritage in a contemporary way. Serving as the waterfront connection linking Downtown, Beacon Hill, Cambridge and the North End, Lovejoy Wharf exemplifies Boston and the live-work-play experience. This limited collection of condominiums provides residents with the natural beauty of the waterfront, a tailored collection of amenities and unparalleled service and hospitality.

Related Beal is proud to introduce Lovejoy Wharf, a home and lifestyle curated expressly for the forward-thinking.

Related Beal invites you to experience Lovejoy Wharf, the missing piece to Boston’s waterfront. Unlocked at the turn of the century, the once concealed wharf has become a beacon to the city. With unimpeded Charles River views, the property is the new standard in waterfront living.
